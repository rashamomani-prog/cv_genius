Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 05, 2026

Welcome to CV app. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our application to create professional resumes.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Data: Email and password provided during registration via Firebase Authentication.
  • CV Content: Information you input such as full name, contact details, work experience, and education to generate your documents.
  • Media: Profile pictures chosen through the app are stored securely in Firebase Storage.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account and synchronize your data across devices.
  • To generate and export professional PDF resumes using our internal PDF Services.
  • To maintain and improve the performance of the app.

3. Permissions Required

The app requires the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage/Gallery: To pick a profile picture and save the generated PDF files to your device.
  • Internet: To communicate with Firebase services and sync your CV data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for backend services, including hosting, database management, and authentication. Your data is processed securely according to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can update or delete your information directly within the app. If you wish to delete your account entirely, all associated data will be removed from our Firebase servers.
